ABOUT

I'm a recent graduate of New York University with a degree in Economics. I grew up in Massachusetts, and now reside in New York.

Currently, I work as a venture partner at Flatiron Investors, where I focus on sourcing and evaluating seed stage technology investment oppotunities. Previously, I interned on the investment team at Metamorphic Ventures, an early stage technology venture capital firm based in New York.

While at NYU, I served on the executive board of Tech@NYU, where I led our Startup Week initiative, planning a week-long entrepreneurship conference attended by over 800 students.

I'm constantly thinking about how technology will shape and unbundle legacy industries, and drive the creation of entirely new industries. I also like donut shops and the Patriots.
